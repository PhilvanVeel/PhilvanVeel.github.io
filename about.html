About

I am an industrial product designer located in Eindhoven in the Netherlands. Currently I am studying my master Industrial Design at the Technical University of Eindhoven. In 2020 I successfully completed the premaster programme, and before that in 2019 I have obtained my Bachelor of engineering at The Hague University of Applied Sciences.

Since I was little I have always been drawing my own ideas of what the future could look like, and it is my dream to give shape to new ways of interacting, feeling and living for people whose lives can be enhanced with new solutions. It would be a privilege to apply my knowledge in the field of design. I Every day I continue to expand my horizons, work hard and be challenged in an inspirational environment. Even when a degree is obtained, designers never cease to be students.

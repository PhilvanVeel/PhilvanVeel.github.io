Design process

Ecomotive is a student team at the University of Technology Eindhoven that innovates in building cars with a low environmental footprint. Where other teams excel at efficiency in driving, Ecomotive focusses on the build process, materials and lifecycle. Often car manufacturers use lightweight composites to increase efficiency, but these materials are toxic, and after the car’s lifecycle has ended, these materials cannot be recycled.

We want to change the automotive industry by proving that cars can be manufactured in a more sustainable way, without compromising the design, use and quality of the vehicle. This year we decided to reach for the stars and archive a CO2 neutral car, by employing smart engineering solutions, partnering up with innovative companies and consulting experts in the field. The car is still currently under development.

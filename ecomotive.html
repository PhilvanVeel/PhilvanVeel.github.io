The evolution of an innovative student team

Meet 'Jane'

Being Chief Design was the biggest, most challenging and most rewarding design experience i've ever had. Working with an interdiscplinary team of over 30 students poses a lot of challenges. It was a pleasure to be involved in an extracurriculair program, in which many students were motivated to create the sustainable car of the future. My role consisted of leading the design team in brainstorm sessions, creating the planning and communicating with the other squad chiefs. Besided these tasks I was also responsible for the external design concept and interior design direction.
